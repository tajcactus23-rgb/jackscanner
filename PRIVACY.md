# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: May 24, 2026**

## Overview

BlueMeanie is designed with privacy as a core principle. This document explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

---

## 📱 Information We Collect

### Device Information
- **Bluetooth MAC Address** - Used only locally to identify detected BLE devices
- **Bluetooth Signal Strength (RSSI)** - Stored to track detection proximity
- **Device Names** - Captured from BLE advertisements for detection purposes

### Location Data
- **Location is NEVER automatically collected**
- If enabled, location is used ONLY to show where detections occurred on your local device
- Location sharing with the community is **disabled by default** and requires explicit opt-in

### Usage Information
- **Detection Count** - Stored locally to track your scanning activity
- **App Settings** - Stored in local preferences

### We Do NOT Collect
- ❌ Personal identification information
- ❌ Contact information
- ❌ Social media data
- ❌ Precise GPS location (unless explicitly enabled)
- ❌ Audio or camera data
- ❌ Content of your communications

---

## 🔒 How We Use Your Information

### Local Processing
All detection data is processed **locally on your device**. We do not transmit detection data to external servers.

### Detection Alerts
- When an Axon device is detected, you receive a local notification
- Notification includes device name and signal strength
- No data is sent when alerts are triggered

### Community Features
If you enable community features:
- **Anonymous usernames** are used by default
- **No personal data** is shared
- You can enable auto-rotate username to change identity every 24 hours
- Detection locations are aggregated and anonymized

### App Improvements
We may collect:
- Anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which screens are visited)
- Crash reports (without personal data)
- Feature usage patterns

---

## 🔐 Data Storage

### Local Storage
- **Detection Database** - Stored in local Room database
- **User Preferences** - Stored in encrypted DataStore
- **No cloud storage** unless you explicitly enable cloud backup (premium feature)

### Data Retention
- Detection history is stored **locally only**
- You can delete all data from the app settings
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data

### Data Security
- All local data is stored securely on your device
- No data is transmitted over unencrypted connections
- We use Android's built-in security features

---

## 👤 Your Rights

### You Can
- ✅ **View all data** collected about your scanning activity
- ✅ **Delete all data** at any time from settings
- ✅ **Disable all tracking** through app settings
- ✅ **Use anonymous mode** by default
- ✅ **Opt out** of any optional data collection
- ✅ **Export your data** for personal backup

### You Control
- 🔧 Location sharing (off by default)
- 🔧 Community features (optional)
- 🔧 Crash reporting (can be disabled)
- 🔧 Auto-rotate username (for enhanced privacy)
- 🔧 Notification preferences

---

## 🚀 Children's Privacy

BlueMeanie is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of child data collection, we will delete it immediately.

---

## 🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. When we do:
- We will update the "Last Updated" date
- We will notify users through the app (if significant changes)
- Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance
